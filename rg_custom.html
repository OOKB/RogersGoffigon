Rogers & Goffigon designs and produces high-end, luxurious, natural fiber textiles using old world weaving tehniques. The company has gained recognition for their refined linens, neutral color paletter and special spinning techniques, including wet-spun linen, giving their fabrics characteristics unique to the industry. The collection has expanded to include fabrics ranging from sheer linens, silks, and wools for window treatments to extremely durable mohair velvets for heavy-use upholstery, most of which is woven and imported from Europe.
